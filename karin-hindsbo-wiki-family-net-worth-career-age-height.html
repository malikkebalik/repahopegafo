Karin Hindsbo Career

In addition to serving as editor-in-chief of the Danish cultural newspaper "Jeblikket," Karin served as the director of a number of art organizations and museums in Norway and Denmark. She served as editor of the art journal Jeblikket from 2003 until 2011. She worked at the Institut for Samtidskunst in Copenhagen from 2002 to 2005, and from 2006 to 2011, she presided over Den Frie Udstillingsbygning. From 2012 to 2014, she served as director of the Srlandets Art Museum in Kristiansand, and from 2014 to 2017, she served as director of the KODE Kunstmuseene in Bergen. She also gave lectures at the University of Copenhagen from 2003 to 2006. Beginning in 2017, she assumed her new role as director of Oslo's National Museum of Art, Architecture, and Design.
