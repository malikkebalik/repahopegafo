Bill Goldbergs Son Is Following in His Footsteps Inside His Life with Wife and Kid

William Scott Goldberg, also known by his ring name Bill Goldberg, is a professional wrestler and former football player best known for his time in World Championship Wrestling (WCW).

Goldberg is currently signed to WWE, where he performs on the RAW brand.

Also a handy actor, he has starred in numerous movies and TV shows, including Universal Soldier: The Return, NCIS: Los Angeles, The Goldbergs, and American Satan.

Speaking of his personal life, the 54-year-old wrestler is a married man. He has been married to Wanda Ferraton Goldberg, a former stunt double, since April 2005.

Goldberg and his wife Ferraton also have a son, Gage, from their longtime marriage.

Meet Bill Goldberg’s Wife Wanda Ferraton

Goldberg’s wife Ferraton, who was born in 1970 in Saskatchewan, Canada, is a former stunt double, television show host, and entrepreneur.

She actively worked as a stunt artist between 2000 and 2005, with credits in movies such as Santa's Slay, Hollywood Flies, Texas Rangers, Caught in the Headlights, Ararat, Christmas Rush, and The Unsaid.

In addition, she starred in 2007's Half Past Dead 2, and made appearances in 2016's TV series documentary Jay Leno's Garage and 2017's television special WrestleMania 33.

Ferraton's Instagram account, where she has over 8900 followers, further reveals that she is the co-owner of Hide and Chic Boutique together with her friend Danielle Kendall.

Her brand apparently sells leather-made purses and bags, most of them ranging below 50 USD.

Bill Goldberg and Wanda Ferraton Listed Their California Home

Goldberg and his spouse lived together at their California home since they got married in 2005.

The house, built in 1997, was purchased by the pro wrestler in 1999 for $1 million.

He also purchased parcels of land nearby and added them to his property, making his house occupy a total of 28 acres of land.

The property includes a four-bedroom, four-bathroom, a large kitchen, family room, airy, vaulted ceilings in the dining room, living room, and office. 

Other amenities of the property include a half-basketball court, a pool and spa with waterfall and slide, a fire pit, and a sitting area.

But in 2019, the couple listed the property for $3.2 million and moved out to be closer to their family in Texas.

Their Son Is Following Bill Goldberg’s Footsteps

Goldberg and Ferraton's son Gage, who was born in May 2006, celebrated his 15th birthday on May 9 this year.

The pro wrestler even took to his Instagram to wish his son on his birthday, as he posted a snap of his wife and his son, writing, "My life in 1 photo. Yesterday #mothersday and today GAGE’s birthday. I love my family more than words can express."

Presently in his freshman year, Gage is an active player on his varsity football team. He represents the team Champions Chargers of his high school, Champion High School.

Doing that, he is certainly following his father's footsteps, as Goldberg was once a champion football player.

He started his football career when he was in school and eventually made it to NFL. He played for Los Angeles Rams, Atlanta Falcons, and Carolina Panthers in NFL.

Gage also seems to have an affection towards wrestling because he made an appearance at Monday Night RAW in August this year with his father.
