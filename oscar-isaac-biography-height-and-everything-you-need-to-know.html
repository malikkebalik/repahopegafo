Oscar Isaac Biography, Height And Everything You Need To Know

Born in Guatemala on March 9, 1979, Oscar Isaac is an American actor, musician, and producer. Oscar Isaac is a man of average height, standing at 5 feet 9 inches (175 cm).

Oscar Isaac was raised in Miami, Florida, after being born in Guatemala. His full name is Oscar Isaac Hernández Estrada, and he has Guatemalan and Cuban parentage.

Oscar Isaac Education

Oscar Isaac graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Drama from the Juilliard School in New York City.

Career: In 2010, Oscar Isaac made his debut with the starring part in the movie “Inside Llewyn Davis.” He has since made appearances in several well-known movies, such as “Ex Machina,” “Star Wars: The Force Awakens,” “The Promise,” and “Annihilation.” He also received praise for his roles in the movies “A Most Violent Year” and the HBO miniseries “Show Me a Hero.” Oscar Isaac is a musician as well as an actor, and he has a record out called “Late Night with Seth Meyers.”

Oscar Isaac Personal life

Oscar Isaac is wed to Danish director Elvira Lind. Together, they are parents to a son and a daughter.

Oscar Isaac Awards and nominations

Throughout his career, Oscar Isaac has received numerous nominations, including those for Golden Globe, Emmy, and Academy Awards. In 2021, he received a Golden Globe for his work in the HBO miniseries “Scenes from a Marriage.”

Oscar Isaac is a supporter of humanitarian causes and has collaborated with groups like UNICEF and the Enough Project to spread awareness of international problems like the refugee crisis and human rights violations in conflict zones.
