Who is Calahan Skogman?

Calahan Skogman is an eminent American actor, screenwriter, theatre, artist, and television personality.

Moreover, he is a basketball player and entrepreneur from Green Bay, Wisconsin, United States.

In addition, he is popular in the entertainment industry for his splendid acting skills and performance.

Calahan Skogman: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity

Calahan Skogman was born in the year 1993, May 13 in Green Bay, Wisconsin, United States. At present, he is 28 years old.

Calahan is an American by nationality and belongs to the pure white Caucasian ethnicity.

Well, he was born into a well-settled Christian family.

His father is “Wade Skogman” who is a businessman, and his mother is “Stefani Skogman” who is a homemaker. Calahan Skogman has a younger brother named Clayton Skogman.

Education, School, University

The talented actor Calahan Skogman completed his schooling at Seymour High School.

After that, he attended the University of Wisconsin. Later, he joined the University of Southern California to pursue a degree in Masters of Fine Arts in acting.

Back then, he also attended some classes at The University of Minnesota Duluth before starring in the professional acting world.

Calahan Skogman: Professional life, Career

Initially, Calahan Skogman was a basketball player for the University of Wisconsin La Crosse team.

After that, he started acting in his college days.

Further, he acted in various theatre productions such as Children of the Sun, Catch Me If You Can, Eurydice, Julius Caesar, The Metal Children, Swimmers, etc.

He came to the highlight after got selected for the main role in the short drama movie “G. I. Jose” (2017).

He played the lead role in the movie ‘Smile’ in2018. Likewise, he starred in a supporting role in the movie “Blood Puppet! Christmas ‘94” in the year 2019.

In addition, Calahan Skogman became popular in the industry after getting the opportunity to perform in the role of Matthias Helvar in Shadow and Bone.
