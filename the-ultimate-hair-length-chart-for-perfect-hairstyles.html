The Ultimate Hair Length Chart for Perfect Hairstyles

Hair Length Chart is a visual guide where lengths of hair are categorized from very short to extremely long. This guide helps an individual identify her hair type and pick the best style for herself.

The ultimate guide to hair length, this chart provides a comprehensive overview of the different types of options that can be explored when it comes to hair styling. It can prove useful in planning your hair care routine and making the right decision.

Short Length Hair

Short Length Hair in the hair chart can range from less than an inch up to 12 inches. The length of the hair basically stops above the shoulder.

This hair length is a fantastic option for those who prefer low-maintenance hair and want a quick and easy styling routine. Short hair is flattering for those with petite facial features since it allows you to showcase your face without being overshadowed by locks.

This versatile hair length includes different types of haircuts that will give you a daring and stylish look. They are listed below:

1. Buzz Cut

The buzz is the shortest haircut in the list of hair length charts for women where hair is shaved very close to the scalp. This creates a bold and minimalist look that exudes a powerful unconventional charm.

The length of the hair in this cut measures up to 1/8 inch or less creating a nearly bald effect. This styling suits women who are confident and daring and bravely looking to experiment with styling to add flair to their appearance.

2. Crew Cut

The Crew Cut is a short and tapered hairstyle that basically features closely trimmed sides and back while leaving longer hair on top. The hair on the crown part of the head is not more than an inch long.

This style in hair lengths chart is considered bold and edgy while also having a graceful touch of femininity. The cut appeals especially to those women who prefer a minimalist and no-fuss style to go on about their daily business.

3. Pixie Cut

Pixie Cut is one of the trendiest hairstyling in the length chart hair for women where the sides and back of the head are neatly trimmed. A slightly longer hair ranging to a few inches is left on the top for styling.

Pixie is also a volumizing haircuts for thin hair for women that can be tailored to suit different face shapes and fashion styles to give you a sleek and elegant look. You can easily adjust the length, add bangs, or incorporate color highlights that leave you room for creative expressions.

4. Short Bob

A chic and timeless haircut, Short Bob is distinguished by a straight and even-length cut that falls between the chin and the shoulders. The bob is usually eight to ten inches in length and beautifully frames the face.

This famous hairstyle particularly suits women with oval or heart-shaped faces since it has the ability to accentuate their facial features and provide a flattering frame. It can further be personalized by incorporating bangs, layers and curls.

5. Chin Length Bob

The Chin Length Bob is a sleek and versatile hairstyle that gives you a timeless and chic appearance to get ready for any event. This versatile style typically features straight or slightly angled lines that frame the face.

While measuring the hair in inches, this haircut involves hair length that typically ranges from eight to twelve inches and can be adjusted according to one's preference. To give yourself a more polished look, you can add bangs and experiment with coloring techniques.

6. Jaw Length Bob

The Jaw Length Bob haircut is a chic and trendy hairstyle for women that involves a straight, blunt cut that beautifully falls just at the jawline. This styling helps to frame the face to offer a clean and sophisticated appearance.

The lengths of hair typically range from 8 to 12 inches and it is customized with different textures to suit personal preference and fashion style. While you opt for this cut, you can go with a side-swept or center parting, add a tousled or wavy texture and try different color options.

7. Shoulder Length Bob

The Shoulder Length Bob is a medium-length haircut in the hair inches chart which is a universally flattering option for a variety of faces. The hair generally falls above the shoulders with a clean cut framing the face.

The hair length can range from ten to fourteen inches allowing enough opportunity for you to get creative with styling to match the theme of the event or gathering. You can experiment with the center or side parting and add layers for extra volume and movement.

Medium Length Hair

Medium Length Hair basically goes from the shoulder to the armpit and measures from 12 to 18 inches for straight strands and up to 24 inches for curly ones.

Medium hair is a popular choice as it provides a perfect balance between the ease of maintenance and the styling possibilities. This timeless choice suits a wide range of face shapes and hair textures which makes it even more versatile.

Whether straight, wavy, or curly, medium-length hair provides a canvas for various styling techniques such as:

1. Collarbone Length

The Collarbone Length haircut is a stylish and versatile choice for women who are looking for a balance of both long and short hairstyling. The mid back hair length is normally kept up to fourteen inches and styled accordingly.

This haircut has the unique ability to maintain a perfect balance between short and long hair and create an appearance that is chic and timeless. Furthermore, some creative additions of layers, bangs, or color highlights can enhance your overall look by giving you the necessary touch of elegance.

2. Armpit Length

The Armpit Length haircut is a versatile hairstyling option for women where you can use the mid-length hair to your advantage to create a flattering appearance. The hair is normally twelve inches in length and frames the face.

This hairstyling does a great job of accentuating your shoulders and neckline to showcase your sense of style. This is a go-to hairdo for women who prefer a medium-length lock and go the extra mile by opting for side-swept or blunt bangs to add creative flair.

3. Bra Strap Length

As the name suggests, the Bra Strap Length haircut is the styling where the hair is cut to a length that just about reaches the level of the bra strap on the back. This style is trendy and versatile which makes it desirable for many.

You can showcase your creativity with this hairstyle by adding subtle layers to provide elegant movement in your hair and create a sleek style. When you have hair of length about 18 inches then you can't just help but incorporate bangs and partition to tailor your look.

Long Length Hair

Long Hair Length has the most variations in terms of length in the Hair Length Chart. It ranges from Mid-Back Length to extra-long Terminal Length which in terms of inches can reach 12 inches or beyond.

Long hair suits a variety of people, enhances femininity and adds versatility to styling options. Its appeal often transcends age and face shape since it can be easily customized to suit individual preferences.

Often associated with a classic and timeless beauty, long hair includes a wide range of hairstyles as mentioned below:

1 Mid Back Length

In the Mid Back Length haircut, the locks are designed to fall to the middle of the back generally around the shoulder blades to create a look that is both elegant and versatile. The hair which is about sixteen inches in length is manageable to be worn with everyday wear.

This style is popular among women of all ages since it suits almost everyone who seeks to create a classic and timeless look. Anyone who opts for this styling option can customize their look ranging from from sleek and polished to tousled and relaxed.

2. Waist Length

In the Waist Length hairstyle, the hair is cut to a dramatic length that reaches the waist of the wearer and styled in a way to create a long and flowing look. The waist hair length typically ranges from thirty to thirty-five inches and complements those with a bohemian style.

If you love long hair and don't mind putting some extra effort into maintaining it regularly then this can be the perfect choice for you. This cut especially suits women with thick and healthy hair since it requires substantial volume to maintain its length and shape. 

3. Hip Length

Women with thick and healthy hair with a love for long locks can opt for a Hip Length Cut. The long hair that gracefully reaches down the hip of the wearer gives a dramatic appearance that is unique in appeal.

The remarkable length of the hair which typically ranges up to thirty-six inches sets it apart from shorter hairstyles to give the wearer a stand-out charm. Creative addition of layers, textures, or even highlights can help to complement your skin tone as well.

4. Tail Bone Length

Tail Bone Lenght haircut is set apart from other hairstyling by its exceptional length which reaches all the way down to the tailbone of the wearer. If are ready to get yourself a dramatic and eye-catching appearance, then this is your ideal choice.

This style best suits women with straight or wavy hair as it helps to highlight the length and health of their locks and adds a touch of glamor to their overall look. The addition of bangs and layers can further give your hair a voluminous appearance while curls or braids help to reflect your unique style.

5. Classic Length

The classic length haircut is a versatile style that has a timeless appeal that has the ability to blend with different types of faces. The hair is cut to a length where it falls to the mid-back or beyond to create a graceful look.

The notable quality of this style is that it is simplicity and elegance help to showcase the personality of the wearer. Experimentation with fringes, bangs and layering techniques allows you to express your creativity while retaining its own enduring appeal.

6. Ankle Length

To achieve ankle-length hairstyling, the hair is extended all the way down to the ankles of the wearer which has a very unconventional appeal to it. This extraordinary length is exceptionally rare which has made it an attention-grabbing style.

Normally, the length of the hair ranges from 36 to 48 inches or even longer depending on the height of the woman. This kind of style is well-suited for those who enjoy showcasing their hair as a defining feature of their appearance.

7. Knee Length

Knee Length haircut is a bold and distinctive hairstyle in the hair length chart which is distinguished by the locks reaching the knees of the wearer. If you see a person wearing this style in public, you are certain to be left in awe.

The long extended hair suits women who are confident, daring, and comfortable with maintaining such an extraordinary length of hair. You can use your hair as a canvas and experiment with different styles, and textures, and also add accessories like braids, beads, or clips to give a personalized touch.

8. Floor Length

The Floor Length cut is an incredibly long and flowing hairstyle where the hair extends all the way down to the floor. It is as dramatic as it is elegant to look at and equally rare which makes it even more special.

The hair extends to an extreme limit reaching several feet which makes it a statement of extraordinary length and luxury. If you are comfortable maintaining your locks then this styling provides you enough room for creativity. 

9. Terminal Length

The Terminal Length is a sleek and classic style for women which is achieved by cutting the hair to a uniform length reaching the very tips of the hair strands. According to personal preference, the length can range from a few inches below the shoulders to waist-length or longer.

For women with healthy long hair, straight or curly, who want a refreshing look with low maintenance, this is an easy pick. It complements various face shapes while the addition of layers and textures or side-swept bangs will give you a classic look with a unique twist.
