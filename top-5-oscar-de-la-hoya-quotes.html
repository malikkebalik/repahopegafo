Top 5 Oscar De La Hoya Quotes

Oscar De La Hoya is a prominent American former professional boxer who has become a boxing promoter in 2002. In 2018, he was renowned as a mixed martial arts (MMA) promoter, and he has also competed as a boxer from 1992 to 2008. He won 11 world titles in six weight classes that include the lineal championship in three weight classes during his career.

Moreover, he is ranked in the 13th position of the best boxer of the all-time list by BoxRec. And the media put his nickname as The Golden Boy of boxing when he played the 1992 Summer Olympics from the United States. He is also a gold medalist of various times.

If you are a great fan of this best boxer, you can visit the following top 5 quotes by him.
