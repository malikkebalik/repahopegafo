Kelly Osbourne Shows off Stunning Weight Loss While Celebrating Her 36th Birthday

Actress and singer Kelly Osbourne, one of the children of Ozzy and Sharon Osbourne, celebrated her 36th birthday by throwing a party and showing her 85-pound weight loss.

On October 27, Kelly turned 36 years old and celebrated the occasion with a birthday party held at the Yamashiro Hollywood restaurant in Los Angeles. The event made headlines for a couple of reasons, including Kelly’s outfit and physique.

The “Project Runway: Junior” judge walked the restaurant’s purple carpet and posed for the photographers rocking a stunning black outfit that really complimented her body.

Kelly Osbourne on March 14, 2019 in Los Angeles, California | Photo: Getty Images

KELLY OSBOURNE’S BEAUTIFUL ENSEMBLE

Kelly opted for a tight, sleeveless, black dress with no sleeves and a deep neckline, showing off some of her tattoos. Apart from her matching high heels, the TV personality wore her lilac-tinted hair in a retro updo. As always, her makeup was on point.

While Kelly’s outfit was indeed gorgeous, it was her slimmer figure that captured everybody’s attention, especially for the confident pose she chose for the pictures.

Ozzy and Sharon Osbourne’s daughter revealed in August 2020 that she had undergone a gastric sleeve surgery sometime in 2018. After that, she lost 85 pounds!

Kelly pointed out that she didn’t care what people said about her esthetic procedure, adding that she was “proud” of it and that it was the best thing she had done.

“The Osbournes” star also revealed that gastric sleeve surgery was not a “quick fix” as it just “moves you in the right direction.” She confessed that if she didn’t work out and eat healthily, she would gain weight again.

[Some men] used to say that she was “great but too fat” to date.

KELLY OSBOURNE GETTING REVENGE ON SEVEN MEN

In late October, Kelly Osbourne appeared in her mom’s talk show “The Talk” to share some details about her weight-loss journey, including that she spent one year going to therapy before actually committing to the procedure.

According to Kelly, the mental transformation had to come first. Apart from that, she explained how she got revenge on seven men who didn’t date her before her surgery.

Kelly Osbourne at her birthday party on October 27, 2020 in Los Angeles, California | Photo: Getty Images

Kelly Osbourne explained that she was interested in those men before undergoing her medical procedure, but all of them used to say that she was “great but too fat” to date.

After shedding some pounds, those people came back into her life trying to start something, but she didn’t give them a chance. Nowadays, Kelly is just “having fun,” so it might be only a matter of time before we see her dating again.
