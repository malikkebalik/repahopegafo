Details of the Accident

The accident occurred on U.S. Highway 30 at County Road 15, also known as Marshall Nursery Road. According to authorities, Rishel’s Jeep Wrangler skidded on the slick road conditions, causing her to lose control and veer into the eastbound lane. It was there that her vehicle collided with a Ford F-150 pickup truck. The Nebraska State Patrol and the Washington County Sheriff’s Office promptly responded to the scene, but unfortunately, their efforts were unable to prevent this tragic outcome.

Rishel’s Background and Contributions

Prior to her role as the Executive Director of the Washington County Community Foundation, Jordan Rishel served as the director for the Blair Chamber of Commerce for an impressive 11 years. Additionally, Rishel also served as a member of the Washington County Board of Supervisors from 2018 to 2022, further showcasing her passion for public service.
