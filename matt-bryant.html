Biography

Matt Bryant was born in Orange, TX on May 29, 1975. NFL kicker best known for his time with teams like the Tampa Bay Buccaneers and Atlanta Falcons. He was named as a Pro Bowl alternate as a member of the Falcons in 2010. He was coached by Jon Gruden during his four-year stint with the Buccaneers.
