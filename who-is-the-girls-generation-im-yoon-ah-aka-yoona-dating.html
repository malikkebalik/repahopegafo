Who Is the Girls' Generation Im Yoon-ah, aka Yoona, dating?

The South Korean entertainment personality, Im Yoon-ah, widely popular as Yoona is popular for portraying a character in Korean dramas and movies. Yoon-ah is also popular as a member of the girl group, Girls' Generation, aka SNSD, who has released successful albums and songs.

The actress, singer, Im Yoon-ah was born in Yeongdeungpo District, Seoul, South Korea, on May 30, 1990. She has an elder sister. Besides, nothing much is known about her parents.

Coming to education, she attended Seoul Shinyoung Elementary School. In 2009, Yoona graduated from Daeyoung High School. Later, she majored in theatre studies at Dongguk University.

Who Is Im Yoon-ah's Boyfriend?

The 30-years-old Yoona has not declared her present relationship status towards the media. She has not mentioned whether she is having any romantic affair or is living a single life.

It seems like the Korean actress currently enjoying her single life. At the moment, Yoona is more focused on her career which might be the reason behind her singleness. Similarly, she might also be searching for the right person to start her love life. 

Relationship With Lee Seung-gi

The KBS Drama Award-winning Yoona was previously in a romantic relationship with Lee Seung-gi, a South Korean singer and actor. Yoona and Lee Seung began their romantic affair in early 2014. Yoona's management agency, SM Entertainment, confirmed the news regarding their relationship saying.

"Lee Seung Gi and YoonA are currently at the phase in which they are getting to know each other."

On the other hand, Lee's management agency, Hook Entertainment, also stated, "Since it is a personal issue, we don’t know the specific details. But it is true that the two are dating. Since both are young and only in the beginning stages, please look upon them fondly."

Everything was going fine. The Korean stars were sharing a perfect bond. However, after a year, Yoona and Lee Seung ended their romantic relationship. The reason behind their separation is unclear, but it is reported that the two's busy schedules lead them to split their relationship. 

Member Of the Girls' Generation

The 5-feet-6-inch (168 cm) tall Yoona's prominence rises to fame after she became a member of the Girls' Generation. The group was formed by South Korea's largest entertainment company SM Entertainment in 2007.

The group is composed of eight members Kim Tae-yeon, Susan Soonkyu Lee, Stephanie Young Hwang, Kim Hyo-Yeon, Kwon Yu-ri, Choi Soo-young, Seo Ju-Hyun, including Yoona.

The K-pop, bubblegum pop, electro-pop, and J pop genres group, Girls' Generation has released nine studio albums. It has released successful songs such as Into the New World, Catch Me If You Can, Lion Heart, New Think, Mr. Taxi, Paparazzi, Galaxy Supernova, etc.

Filmography

Yoona started her acting career by playing the role of Shin Joo-young from the 2007 MBC TV series, Two Outs in the Ninth Inning. Till now, she has made her appearance in drama series such as Woman of Matchless Beauty, Park Jung-geum, Cinderella Man, The King in Love, The King in Love, Hush, Prime Minister & I, etc.

Similarly, Yoona made a film debut by working in the 2012 documentary film I Am. Likewise, she played Park Min-young in the 2017 action film Confidential Assignment. The actor Hyun Bin has also starred in the movie.

Moreover, Yoona has played a leading role in other South Korean films Exit and Miracle. Furthermore, Im Yoon-ah will also play in the Confidential Assignment's sequel, Confidential Assignment 2: International.

Net Worth

The South Koreana entertainment personality Im Yoon-ah has an estimated net worth of around $2 million (2,271,320,000 KRW). The primary source of earning for Yoona is from her acting, singing career.

As per Salary Expert, the average salary of an actress in South Korea is $28,327.79 (32,156,839 KRW). But Yoona might be learning more than an average as she is an experienced and popular actress all over South Korea. 

Besides, Yoon-ah also works as a model. She has made her modeling deals with various fashion companies, brands such as Jigott (clothing brand), Miu Miu, etc. Similarly, she has appeared as a cover model in fashion, lifestyle magazines like Marie Claire, L'Officiel, and others.
