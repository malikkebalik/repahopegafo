BlocBoy JB Career

In 2012, JB started putting out music on the musical platform SoundCloud. In 2016, he released his first mixtape, Who Am I. The mixtape included a track called “No Chorus Pt. 6,” which gained over two million views on YouTube. In October 2017, he was involved in a controversy after posting a video of himself shooting dice at Hamilton High School.

In early 2018, he released the song “Shoot,” which created a dance craze called the “shoot dance.” Following that, he also released another single, “Rover.” The song went viral and caught the attention of Canadian rapper Drake.
