Drakes Son Scores LaMelo Ball Puma Sneakers After Drawing NBA Star

Drake‘s son, Adonis, has received an exclusive care package from Puma with a rare pair of LaMelo Ball sneakers that will have him ready for his next hoop session.

The 6 God posted the clip of Adonis unboxing the Puma X LaMelo Ball MB.01 Digital Camo kicks to his Instagram Story on Monday (May 8).

AD LOADING...

An excited Adonis can be seen running to his room and losing his mind when he discovers the orange-trimmed sneakers designed by one of his favorite NBA players in the Charlotte Hornets all-star.

“Oh yeah! Let’s go,” he repeats while gazing at the kicks and running around his bedroom while mixing in some Spanish. “I got LaMelo’s!”

Drake captioned the moment with a series of laughing emojis: “This guy!”

“My nephew really love the game,” he wrote.
