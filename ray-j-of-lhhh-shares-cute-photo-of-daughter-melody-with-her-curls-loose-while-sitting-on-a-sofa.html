Ray J of LHHH Shares Cute Photo of Daughter Melody with Her Curls Loose While Sitting on a Sofa

Ray J won't stop gushing about his adorable daughter, and we are here for every picture!

The new parents, Ray J and Princess Love, can't get enough of their first creation, the beautiful Melody, and we can't either. Melody's photos are everywhere on the couple's Instagram, and she even has her personal Instagram account!

Ray J. poses backstage in the press room during the 2019 Black Music Honors at Cobb Energy Performing Arts Center | Photo: Getty Images

In the recent snapshot that Ray J posted on Instagram, the little girl looked incredibly adorable in a white onesie and pretty curls.

Melody sat relaxingly on a couch, and it appears that she might have been watching TV; our best guess is a cartoon! The cute girl seemed captivated by whatever it is she was viewing.

By the rapper's caption, it's clear that he was also fascinated by her, as he wrote:

"My mood : @melodylovenorwood"

Melody, who was born on May 23, 2018, marked her first birthday this year, as reported by Heavy. The famous parents expressed their wishes for their daughter in two loving Instagram posts each.

On whether they are ready for baby number 2, the couple is on the green!

Princess Love (L) and Ray J attend the 2019 BET Awards at Microsoft Theater | Photo: Getty Images

Ray J and Princess Love disclosed news about expecting their second baby in January 2020.

On Sunday, the stunning family uploaded a slideshow of family pictures to announce the latest update

“Baby #2 on the way! @MelodyLoveNorwood your [sic] about to be a big sister! #Godisthegreatest.”

As seen in Princess Love's Instagram post, the lovely family were in an all-white ensemble, with Melody wearing a t-shirt that read, “big sis.”

The excited mother explained with a caption that read:

“Somebody’s gonna be a big sis ??? New addition arriving Jan 2020 @melodylovenorwood @rayj#2under2 #Blessed.”
