Biography

John W. Bubbles was born in Kentucky on February 19, 1902. American stage and screen actor and vaudeville performer who appeared in such stage productions as the Ziegfeld Follies and Porgy and Bess and in such films as Varsity Show, A Song Is Born, and Cabin in the Sky. Michael Jackson‘s pet chimpanzee, Bubbles, was named after John W. Bubbles. He and Ford “Buck” Washington formed a performance duo called Buck and Bubbles.
