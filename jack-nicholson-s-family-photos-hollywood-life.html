Jack Nicholsons Family: Photos Hollywood Life

Jack Nicholson is a proud dad to five, including Ray’s siblings Jennifer, Caleb, Honey, and his full-sister Lorraine. Jack’s eldest daughter Jennifer was born in 1963 with his only ex-wife Sandra Knight, who he met on the set of ‘The Terror.’ The couple divorced in ’68, and Jack went on to become a father via several other partners.

His next son Caleb was born in 1970 via Susan Anspach, however, Caleb was later legally adopted by actor Mark Goddard. In later years, Jack acknowledged Caleb was his son and the pair were able to develop a relationship.

The Oscar winner went on to welcome daughter Honey in 1981 with Danish model Winnie Hollman. Honey has maintained her privacy throughout most of her life, which has been spent in Denmark. She’s since moved to Ireland with her husband and three children, where she owns a pub.

Finally, Jack is dad to daughter Lorraine, born 1990, and son Ray, born in 1992, via ex partner Rebecca Broussard. Both of his younger kids have pursued careers in acting, just like him.
