Boise Airport unveils new parking garage, ramping up capacity amid record-breaking passenger numbers

BOISE, Idaho (CBS2) — The long-awaited public parking garage at the Boise Airport opened today following a ribbon cutting ceremony.

Dubbed the new East Parking Garage, long-term parking will see a significant increase in capacity, adding over 1,100 additional stalls.

BOI reported a record year for passenger travel in 2022, serving nearly 4.5 million passengers, and 2023 appears to be on pace to break that record again. There are other projects that are planned, or currently under construction at the Boise Airport, including an employee parking garage planned to open next spring, a consolidated rental car center that will break ground this fall, and a brand new concourse for the airport.
