Leomie Anderson- Professional Career

The most intriguing thing about the model is that she is also a YouTuber. Yes, it’s difficult to imagine, but she began on YouTube on November 1, 2012, when she was 19 years old. She enjoys providing cosmetic tutorials and teaching others about her way of life.

“She may share her personality and attitude with the rest of the world through vlogging and a YouTube channel.”

Leomie must replicate someone else’s character on the runway, including hair, makeup, and other aspects. Vlogging and making videos, on the other hand, have become her new platform for expressing herself and her interests.

The model started filming movies for fun, but people started noticing her and she embraced the attention. Her vlog covers all of her hobbies and interests, as well as beauty, fashion, and travel. Furthermore, in “Cracked China Cup,” she creates Fashion Week diaries to enlighten people about what happens behind the scenes.

So far, the model has uploaded 75 videos. Her fans have been there for her ever since. She currently has 9,334 followers.

Leomie’s modeling career began when she was 14 years old, on her walk home from school in London. The plumped lip model began her modeling career in 2010. Her first fashion presentation was with Marc Jacobs, a well-known American designer.

Among others, she has walked the runways for Moschino, Tom Ford, Chloé, Yeezy, Fenty Puma, Calvin Klein, Giorgio Armani, Oscar de la Renta, Ralph Lauren, and Tommy Hilfiger. On April 4, 2019, she was named the newest Victoria’s Secret Angel.
