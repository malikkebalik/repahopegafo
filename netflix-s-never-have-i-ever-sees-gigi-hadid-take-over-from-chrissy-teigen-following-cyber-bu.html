Netflixs Never Have I Ever Sees Gigi Hadid Take Over from Chrissy Teigen Following Cyber-bu

Gigi Hadid has stepped in to replace Chrissy Teigen on the Netflix show “Never Have I Ever.”

Chrissy decided to step down from the TV role last month amidst allegations of cyber-bullying.

At 26, Gigi is narrating for the character of Paxton Hall-Yoshida, a role originally intended for Chrissy.

In a promo clip shared online, Gigi is heard saying: “This is Paxton Hall-Yoshida. He’s a 16-year-old boy from Sherman Oaks, California. And I am model, designer, activist, and a former 16-year-old from California, Gigi Hadid.”

Gigi posted the promo clip on Instagram with the caption: “Had the BEST time getting to narrate a new episode of @neverhaveiever — I got your back, Pax! Check out Season 2, NOW on @netflix !!!!”

Chrissy withdrew from the voice role on the second season of the Netflix series in June this year.
