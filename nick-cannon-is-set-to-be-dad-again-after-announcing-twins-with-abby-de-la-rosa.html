Nick Cannon Is Set to Be Dad Again after Announcing Twins with Abby De La Rosa

Recently, Nick Cannon’s girlfriend Abby De La Rosa announced that they are expecting twin boys.

The mom-to-be shared photos from her maternity shoot, flaunting a beautiful ensemble with pearls draping her torso with a long white train as she leaned against her baby daddy. 

Cannon, shirtless, had his tattoos on full display, as he wore a pair of black Cannon boxers with matching pants and a turban. 

The short-haired beauty wrote a heartfelt caption dedicated to her twin boys where she expressed her gratitude to God for not one but two babies and assured the boys that she and Nick will always be there for them. She also enthused they cannot wait to meet them. 

A Look Into Nick’s Children

Nick Cannon definitely has his hands full as a father. In 2008, the Wild ‘n Out star tied the knot with Mariah Carey in the Bahamas.

On their third wedding anniversary, the couple welcomed their fraternal twin son Moroccan and daughter Monroe Scott which Cannon confirmed in a tweet. 

However, the pair called it quits following which Cannon got into a relationship with Brittany Bell, a former NBA dancer for the Phoenix Suns, model, and pageant titleholder. The two were reportedly dating sometime around 2015, just a year before Nick’s divorce from Carey was finalized.

In 2017, the Cannon and Bell couple welcomed their first child, a son named Golden “Sagon” Cannon. In a sit-down with Power 106 hosts J Cruz and Krystal Bee, Cannon opened up about his family. 

 I’ve always wanted five kids. Just because my marriage was over didn’t mean I couldn’t continue my family.

And Cannon kept true to his words when he had his fourth child, a daughter with Bell, in 2020. The former Miss Arizona USA said their daughter was the best gift ever in a festive Instagram post on December 26, 2020.

The duo was in an on-and-off relationship from the very beginning. Although it is not clear when the pair split up, Cannon was seeing Jessica White when he welcomed his daughter. Cannon and White subsequently split following the news.

Nick was also romantically linked to Lanisha Cole in February. But now, the Masked Singer host is ready to be a father again with Rosa and the twins. 

Cannon Does Not Believe in Monogamy

The America’s Got Talent host wears many hats as a producer, rapper, host, actor, but fans seem to love keeping up with his relationships. He’s been linked to several notable women over the years.

On his own radio show Nick Cannon Mornings, he got candid about his relationship with reality star Evelyn Lozada. He revealed he could not subscribe to the idea of monogamy because men rarely had it in their DNA.

It’s that hunting mentality, but it’s also like ‘yo if we suppose to be fruitful and multiply…. there’s not many creatures on earth that are monogamous.

Cannon confessed he loves the romantic and whimsical facet of love, but once a woman wants to live together, he gets anxious.
