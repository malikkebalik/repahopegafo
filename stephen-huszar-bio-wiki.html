Stephen Huszar Bio (Wiki)

Who is Stephen Huszar?

Stephen Huszar is a Canadian film and television actor. People mostly know him for his role as Yorkie in the TV series ‘Letterkenny’.

Additionally, Stephen Huszar has also appeared in ‘iZombie’, ‘Shadowhunters: The Mortal Instruments’, ‘Supernatural’, and ‘Cashing In’.

Stephen Huszar: Age, Parents, Ethnicity

Huszar was born in Canada, on 24 Jan 1974. He developed an interest in acting became from an early age. He is of Canadian nationality and Caucasian ethnicity.

Furthermore, there are no details available relating to his parents.

In which school did Stephen Huszar attend?

Talking about his education, Huszar attended Aden Bowman Collegiate.

Additionally, Stephen Huszar also studied at the University of Saskatchewan.

Stephen Huszar: Career, Salary, Net Worth

Stephen Huszar initially appeared in the TV movie ‘The Cradle Will Fall’ in 2004. In addition, he played the role of Officer Duncan Finney in ‘Caught in the Headlights’.

In 2006, he also had roles in ‘Lesser Evil’, ‘Dark Storm’, and ‘Shock to the System’.

Since then, Stephen Huszar has appeared in numerous other movies and television series. All in all, he has over 40 credits as an actor.

Some other movies and television series that Huszar has appeared in are ‘Return to Christmas Creek’, ‘Hometown Holiday’, ‘Carter’, ‘Magical Christmas Ornaments’, ‘The Flash’, ‘The Rooftop Christmas Tree’, ‘Milton’s Secret’, ‘Spun Out’, ‘Backstrom’, ‘Aurora Teagarden Mystery: A Bone to Pick’, ‘Girlfriends’ Guide to Divorce’, ‘The Guerilla Picture Show’, ‘Jackhammer’, ‘Fringe’, ‘Faces in the Crowd’, ‘12 Men of Christmas’, ‘Smallville’, ‘Paradise Falls’, and ‘The Wedding Wish’ among others.

Huszar has not revealed his current salary. Additionally, there are no details available about his net worth.
