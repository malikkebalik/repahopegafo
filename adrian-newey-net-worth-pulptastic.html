Adrian Newey: British Formula One Engineer with a Net Worth of $50 Million

Adrian Newey is a renowned British Formula One engineer who has a net worth of $50 million. He is widely recognized as the chief technical officer of the Red Bull Racing Formula One team and earns an annual salary of $10.2 million. Born in Stratford-upon-Avon, Warwickshire, England in December 1958, Adrian has worked as a race engineer, designer, aerodynamicist, and technical director for Formula One and IndyCar.

Newey is considered one of the top engineers in Formula One, with his designs winning over 150 Grand Prixs and several titles. He has won 10 Constructors’ Championships, which is more than any other designer. Adrian is the only designer who has won the titles with three different Formula One teams, and he has won them with six different drivers.

Before joining Red Bull Racing in 2007, Newey designed championship Formula One cars for McLaren and Williams F1. His cars won the Formula One drivers’ and constructors’ championships from 2010 to 2013. Additionally, he designed cars that won the 1985 and 1986 CART titles.
