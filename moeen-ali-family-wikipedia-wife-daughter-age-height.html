Moeen Ali Wikipedia

Moeen Ali Wikipedia:- Moeen Munir Ali OBE was born on 18 June 1987 in Birmingham, United Kingdom. He is an English cricketer who serves as the vice-captain for England in limited-overs cricket. He played Test cricket for England between 2014 and 2023 when he became only the 16th man to score 3,000 runs and take 200 wickets in Tests. He represents Warwickshire in domestic cricket. Ali signed for Warwickshire aged just 15 and scored a half-century for the county's second XI just days before his 16th birthday. After more games at this level in 2004, and a first outing for the England Under-19s against their Bangladeshi counterparts, he spent a successful winter playing for the Under-19s on a tour to India.
