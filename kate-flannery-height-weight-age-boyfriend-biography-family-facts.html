Kate Flannery is an American actress, theatre artist, musician, singer, dancer, and voiceover artist who is best known for playing Meredith Palmer in the NBC mockumentary series The Office. In addition to that, she has also been cast in roles like Bunny in Spyder Games, Carol in OK K.O.! Let’s Be Heroes, Principal Saperstein in All Night, Georgina in Summer Camp Island, Crossing Guard Sandy in American Housewife, Anne Sullivan in Another Period, Ann Russo in Kevin (Probably) Saves the World, Nora in Young Sheldon, Barb Miller in Steven Universe, Norma in Helicopter Mom, Elaine Finkle in Wizards of Waverly Place, Rosemary in At the Devil’s Door, Karen in 4th Man Out, “Mean” Marge Bronigan in Brooklyn Nine-Nine, and Marla in Danny Roane: First Time Director.

    Kate Flannery Facts

  • She spent her childhood years growing up in the suburb of Ardmore, Pennsylvania.
  • She has worked as a waitress at Kate Mantelini restaurant in Beverly Hills.
  • From 2005 to 2013, Kate Flannery brought to life the character of Meredith Palmer, a socially inappropriate and s*xually promiscuous representative at the Dunder Mifflin Scranton branch, in The Office.
  • Alongside Adam Pally, Sarah Burns, Reid Scott, Catherine Reitman, and Kevin Dunn, she co-starred as Principal Miller in the romantic comedy film, Slow Learners, in 2015.
  • Kate Flannery played Susan in the 2018 comedy-drama film, Fishbowl California.
  • She was cast as Nora in an episode titled A Tummy Ache and a Whale of a Metaphor of the CBS period sitcom, Young Sheldon, in January 2019.
  • Under the pseudonym “Lady Puff”, she was a member of the band Mono Puff.
  • In 2019, Kate Flannery appeared as a contestant on the 28th installment of Dancing with the Stars, a popular ABC network dance competition series. She was partnered with first-time professional Pasha Pashkov and was eliminated 5th.
  • She has been friends with actress, comedian, and author Jane Lynch. Also, Kate was featured in Jane Lynch’s Christmas album A Swingin’ Little Christmas which was ranked at number #8 on the Billboard top 100 charts.
  • Kate has done voiceover work for video games like OK K.O.! Lakewood Plaza Turbo and OK K.O.! Let’s Play Heroes.
