With a fashion fight, Cardi B and Nicki Minaj continue their longstanding feud. Cardi B is releasing flames! Online, a winter wonderland of fashion hysteria has broken out, setting Cardi B and Nicki Minaj against one another and setting up an exciting confrontation.

The WAP singer doesn’t appear to be taking the chilly comparisons lightly. In a furious outburst, the rapper addressed Nicki Minaj’s admirers with passion, addressing claims that she was imitating the Barbz queen’s style.

This happened after it was observed that both artists had matching white extensions and were donning white fur coats.

What did Cardi B have to say to Nicki’s slew of fans?

Cardi B fights back in a winter-style confrontation after being compared to Nicki Minaj.

On December 23rd, Cardi B posted a gorgeous picture of herself with similar white extensions and an ethereal white fur coat. She had no idea that Nicki Minaj had just shown off a similar look.

The fans didn’t take long to launch a social media comparison between the two rappers’ attire. “You mo–king fans and bi*** are arguing over a coat that I had on for 45 seconds… from my house to my car,” the Bongos singer wrote on her Instagram story on Saturday.

She went on to say that the only reason she wore that “old a**” coat was because it was made by her aunt and is part of her archival collection.

“I don’t care about these individuals the way you all seem to care about me,” she asserted, probably referring to Nicki Minaj.

The rant didn’t end there; the 31-year-old rapper continued, stating that she doesn’t pay attention to these individuals. “They can’t dress compared to me.” She further expressed, “Damn, Fu**** can’t enjoy her life.”

How did Nicki Minaj react to the exaggerated theatrics of her fans?

In response to Cardi B’s criticism of winter fashion, Nicki Minaj, the Barbie World vocalist, posted a photo of herself wearing an identical fur coat on Instagram, tagging the picture with a lyric from her song.

As the caption for the photo, she wrote, “B!***es Jackin,” using the lyrics to her song “Barbie Dangerous” from “Pink Friday 2.” “I still Queenin.”

Cardi B and Nicki Minaj’s feud

Since Cardi B’s career began in 2017, there has been a persistent feud between her and Nicki Minaj.

For nearly five years, the two singers have been pursuing each other. When Nicki Minaj criticized Cardi B’s husband, Offset, in May 2017, a spark was ignited.

All of this culminated in their legendary altercation at a fashion show. When Nicki’s husband was sent under house arrest in 2023 due to threats against Offset, the story took a dark turn.
